Parametrics and Generation

Description:

For this project, we are tasked with developing a daily practice of "code sketches." Rather than constructing a single project, our goal is to allot daily time to exploring generative art. With our assigned letter as your inspiration, create 21 code “sketches” of that letter in p5 over the course of 3 weeks.

I chose K as my sketch letter. After 21 days, I completed the sketches below. They are divided into three categories, static, dynamic and interactive sketches.
