My intent:

At this stage, I adopted the opinions that everyone gave me, so I first drafted a form within a time span of one week. Phase4

,

My intent:

This is my final “Me of the Screen”. Due to the size of the text, I divided the infographics into three parts, in addition, consisting of static pictures and dynamic videos.

In the final division, I divided it into six sections, the first is about IMDb's watched and added to watch list features; the second is about how often and where I use the app every day; the third is about what I like the keywords searched in the search section; the fourth is the short reviews and ratings I have written for watched movies; the fifth is which categories of videos I tend to watch in the video section. Finally, the most important feature that IMDb brings to me is that it can show a movie in all aspects. It can show a movie from the director, actors, promotional films and stills, giving me a lot of objective evaluations, so that I could easily find a movie I like.
